Privacy Policy

We keep this simple. Here is what you should know about how HireWhen works and how data is handled.

Information we collect

HireWhen does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The numbers you enter into the calculator are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to a server. We do not have user accounts, forms that submit data, or any backend that stores your inputs.

Cookies and advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to personalize the ads you see based on your browsing activity. These cookies are set by Google, not by HireWhen.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. You can also manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-party services

HireWhen uses Google AdSense to serve ads. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet. For more information about how Google collects and uses data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party tracking, analytics, or data collection services are used on this site.

Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to request, correct, or delete on our end. If you have concerns about cookies placed by Google, you can manage those through your browser or via the Google Ad Settings link above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at hello@hirewhen.com.
